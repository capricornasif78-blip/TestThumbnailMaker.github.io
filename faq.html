Can I use thumbnails for commercial projects?
Yes — templates created here are free to use, but check image licenses if you upload third-party images.
Do thumbnails download in HD?
Yes — exports are PNG at the canvas resolution (1280x720 by default).
Is there a refund for Premium?
This demo doesn't process payments. For a real product implement a refund policy via your payment provider.
